Hydro—Hegemony
Water Conflict Chronology
Release: January 2023
Volume: 378 pages
Format: 23 × 29,5 × 3 cm
Water is life,
yet water is inherently political. As cities expand, urban populations are increasingly in competition with farmers and industry for access to water. Hydro-hegemony consists of showcasing water conflicts from 3000 BC right until the 21st century, demonstrating how water is the lifeblood of ecosystems; global environmental change, particularly climate change, and is further intensifying competition among rival uses and users, leading to conflicts and war.
